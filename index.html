Who We Are

About Eco Shastra

Eco Shastra is the official Economics Society of Sri Aurobindo College (Evening), University of Delhi. The society aims to promote economic awareness, analytical thinking, leadership, creativity and student engagement through workshops, competitions, seminars and flagship events.

We provide students with opportunities to learn beyond the classroom and connect economics with real-world applications — bridging theory with practice, academics with industry.

Mission

To create an engaging platform where students can explore economics, develop practical skills and grow as future leaders.

Vision

To become one of the most active and impactful student societies in Delhi University.
