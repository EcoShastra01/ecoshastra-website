About the Society

Where economic thought
meets action.

Eco Shastra is the official Economics Society of Sri Aurobindo College (Evening), University of Delhi.

The society promotes academic excellence, economic awareness, leadership development, creativity, and student engagement through events, discussions, workshops, competitions, and collaborative initiatives.

Our Mission

A platform to learn, grow, and lead.

To create a platform where students can learn, grow, network, and develop a deeper understanding of economics and the world around them.

How We're Organised

Departments

Eight teams, each responsible for a distinct part of how Eco Shastra runs and shows up.

Content Department

Crafts the research, articles, and written material that carry Eco Shastra's intellectual voice.

Graphics Department

Designs the visual identity behind every poster, post, and campaign the society puts out.

Public Relations

Builds and maintains relationships with faculty, partners, and other societies on Eco Shastra's behalf.

Marketing Department

Plans outreach and drives visibility for every event and initiative across platforms.

Human Resources

Manages internal coordination, member engagement, and team welfare within the society.

Creative Department

Shapes the concepts and creative direction behind events, themes, and campaigns.

Social Media

Runs Eco Shastra's online presence, keeping members and followers updated in real time.

Event Management

Plans and executes the society's events from concept through to closing, on ground and online.
